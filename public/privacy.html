Privacy Policy for Luminora

Last updated: July 3, 2026

At Luminora, we prioritize the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by Luminora and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Account Information: When you register an account, we collect your name, email address, and account credentials.
  • Biometric Data (Facial Verification): If you choose to enable Face Authorization, we use FaceIO (a third-party biometric service) to capture and verify your facial features. Your actual biometric face templates are processed securely by FaceIO and are not stored directly on our servers.
  • Academic Data: Tasks, schedules, study notes, and doubts that you submit to the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our educational platform.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services.
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account using credential check and optional biometric verification.
  • Respond to your queries and provide customer support.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools to assist with our operations:

  • Supabase: For backend database services and user credential management.
  • FaceIO: For secure facial biometric verification and liveness checks. Please refer to FaceIO's Security & Privacy Policy for details on how they protect biometric signatures.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you log in, submit, or access your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
